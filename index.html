About the Author

Shlama Boudagh is a passionate storyteller with a love for both the written word and the game of soccer... He is currently pursuing his Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Michigan – Flint.

Proudly Chaldean/Assyrian, Shlama draws inspiration from his heritage and the values instilled by his parents, Khairy and Suereth Boudagh. He is the eldest of four siblings—Shayna, Yona, and Shupra—whose encouragement fuels his creative and academic ambitions.
