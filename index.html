About the Author

Shlama Boudagh is a passionate storyteller with a love for both the written word and the game of soccer. When he’s not crafting suspenseful narratives, he can be found coaching on the field, sharing his dedication and energy with his players. He is currently pursuing his Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Michigan – Flint, driven by a fascination for design and problem-solving.

Proudly Chaldean/Assyrian, Shlama draws inspiration from his heritage and the values instilled by his parents, Khairy and Suereth Boudagh. He is the eldest of four siblings—Shayna, Yona, and Shupra—whose encouragement fuels his creative and academic ambitions. Through both his writing and his life, Shlama strives to blend discipline, creativity, and community into everything he does.
